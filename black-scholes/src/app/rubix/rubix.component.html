A Planar Rubix Cube

Based on an animation I found on twitter, and was unable to verify the original creator.

Uses a CanvasRenderingContext2D, and a whole bunch of matrix calculations, to present an accurate, and complete projection of a rubix cube into a plane. Any algorithms you could use on a traditional rubix cube will be matched 1:1 by this representation.

How to play:

    Left click: Rotate ring counter clockwise
    Right click: Rotate ring clockwise
